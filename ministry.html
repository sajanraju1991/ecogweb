Young people's Endeavour- YPE

As the Y.P.E. team, we are committed to mentoring and guiding the youth in their journey and using their talent to glorify God and to build His kingdom. Y.P.E. is conducted every third Sunday at 12:00 PM. The mission of Y.P.E. in Ebenezer Church of God (E.C.O.G.), Addlestone, is to teach our youth in the fear, knowledge, and wisdom of God and to be true disciples of Jesus Christ. This enables them to kindle an inspiration within them to spread the incomparable love of Christ in this society. We believe that God has a definite plan and purpose for every individual and through the power of the Holy Spirit. God has a purpose for everyone. Therefore, we invite the youth to participate in their calling in Jesus Christ.
