Ebenezer Church of God

Ebenezer Church of God is a Pentecostal community where we demonstrate the unconditional love of God through our ministry in Ebenezer Church of God, Addlestone. Our church is a body of Christ where the power of the Holy Spirit is manifested. We proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ.


"Remember Jesus Christ, raised from the dead, descended from David. This is my gospel."
2 Timothy- 2:8
